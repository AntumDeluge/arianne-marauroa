Title  : Attributes
Info   : Description of the attributes
Keyword: attributes
Author : Miguel Angel Blanch Lardin
Created: 2003/12/22
status : planning

Attributes are the containers of information in Marauroa.
They are designed to contains strings, because they are the most flexible.

We have added also support for List of Strings. They are encoded as:
	name=[value|...|value]
	
Try to keep the names as short as possible.
